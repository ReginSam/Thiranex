Welcome to the portfolio website ("Site"). I am committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how I collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit this website.

Information I Collect

I collect information you voluntarily provide when you:

This information may include your name, email address, phone number, and any message content you provide. This information is used to respond to your inquiries and maintain communication.

Automatically Collected Information

When you access this Site, I may automatically collect certain information about your visit, including:

I use the information I collect to:

I do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. I may share information only in the following circumstances:

I implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

Data Retention

I retain personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected. Contact form submissions are retained for 2 years. Newsletter subscribers can unsubscribe at any time.
