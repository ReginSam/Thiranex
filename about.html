About Me

My Journey

I'm a passionate full-stack developer dedicated to creating accessible, semantic web experiences. With over 5 years of professional experience, I've specialized in building inclusive digital products that serve users of all abilities.

My journey into web development began with a deep appreciation for the web's universal nature. I believe that accessibility is not an afterthought but a core principle of great design and development. Every project I undertake is built with WCAG 2.1 compliance as a foundation.
